# China Market Micro-Launch Pack

I help small product teams adapt English product pages, app listings, and SaaS landing pages for Chinese-speaking buyers.

This is a compact paid service for founders, Shopify sellers, app developers, and small SaaS teams who want practical China-market launch assets without hiring a full agency.

## Packages

### Starter - USD 29

Best for testing demand.

- Chinese product title and subtitle
- 5 localized selling points
- 3 RedNote/Xiaohongshu post angles
- 10 Chinese keywords or tags
- 1 short FAQ section

Delivery: 1-2 days after payment and required product context.

### Standard - USD 79

Best for a real launch page or store listing.

- Full Chinese product page rewrite
- 10 RedNote/Xiaohongshu post ideas
- 5 competitor or comparable-product notes
- Buyer objections and answer angles
- FAQ, CTA, and short ad copy variants
- 1 revision

Delivery: 2-3 days.

### Sprint - USD 149

Best for founders who need a compact go-to-market kit.

- Everything in Standard
- 20 RedNote/Xiaohongshu content ideas
- 5 short-form video hooks
- Keyword and hashtag table
- One-page China launch memo in PDF/DOCX
- 2 revisions

Delivery: 3-5 days.
